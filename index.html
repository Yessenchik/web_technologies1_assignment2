What is Bazarda?

Bazarda is a community-driven marketplace that connects nearby buyers and sellers. We focus on trust (verified users), speed (instant chat), and security (escrow-style wallet).

Trust & Safety

Profile badges, dispute history, and clear rules reduce risk.

Instant Chat

Message the seller right away and pin important details.

Wallet & Receipts

Top up balance, pay in one tap, download receipts for each order.

How it works

  1. Create account: verify email and complete profile.
  2. List or search: post with photos and fair pricing — or explore categories.
  3. Chat & deal: discuss details in chat, agree on delivery/pickup.
  4. Pay safely: use wallet; funds release after confirmation.

Is Bazarda free?

Browsing and listing are free. Small fee applies on successful sales.

Can I get a receipt?

Yes. Every wallet payment generates a downloadable receipt.
